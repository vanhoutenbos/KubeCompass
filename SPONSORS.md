## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I sponsor anonymously?**  
A: Yes! We'll acknowledge "anonymous sponsor" without details.

**Q: Do you accept one-time donations?**  
A: Yes, but recurring sponsorship helps us plan infrastructure costs.

**Q: What if my tool gets a bad review?**  
A: Sponsorship doesn't influence reviews. If your tool needs improvement, we'll be honest about it.

**Q: Can I sponsor specific testing (e.g., only my tool)?**  
A: No. Sponsorship supports general infrastructure, not specific tool promotion.

**Q: How do I know funds are used properly?**  
A: Monthly/quarterly reports show exactly how infrastructure is used. All spending is transparent.

**Q: Can I sponsor with services instead of money?**  
A: Absolutely! Cloud credits, CI infrastructure, hosting are all valuable in-kind sponsorship.

**Q: Will you prioritize my tool for testing?**  
A: We may test it sooner if there's community interest, but priority is based on user needs, not sponsorship.

**Q: Can I get early access to reviews?**  
A: Gold tier sponsors get early access to content, but editorial approval still required.
