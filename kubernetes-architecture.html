üï∏Ô∏è Service Mesh - The difference

A Service Mesh is a special layer that lives between your Services. It intercepts all traffic between microservices and adds functionality such as:

  • Mutual TLS (mTLS) encryption between all services
  • Advanced load balancing and traffic management
  • Circuit breaking and retry logic
  • Distributed tracing for observability
  • Metrics and logging without code changes
